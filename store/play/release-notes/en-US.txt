Inukshuk 1.0 — offline trail navigation with your own maps.

• Import georeferenced PDF maps, overlaid on live GPS
• OpenStreetMap base layer when you're off the map
• Record trails with live time, distance and elevation (D+/D-)
• Save and share routes as GPX
• Elevation profile for every recorded trail
• Foreground-only location — nothing tracked in the background

Thanks for testing! Please send feedback on map import and GPS accuracy.
