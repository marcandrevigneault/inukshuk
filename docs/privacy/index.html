Inukshuk — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Inukshuk is a free, open-source app for offline trail navigation: it displays georeferenced PDF maps with your GPS position and records your routes as GPX files. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data. The short version: your data stays on your device.

Who we are

Inukshuk is developed by Marc-André Vigneault. For any privacy question, contact: marcandre.vigneault.96@gmail.com.

Data the app uses

  • Location (GPS). With your permission, the app reads your device location while it is open to show where you are on the map and, when you start a recording, to build your trail. Location is processed on your device only. It is not sent to us or to any server, and it is not used for advertising or analytics.
  • Files you import and create. PDF maps you import and the GPX tracks you record are stored locally on your device, inside the app's private storage. They are not uploaded anywhere. You can delete any map or track inside the app at any time, and uninstalling the app removes all of them.

Data we collect

None. Inukshuk has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no crash-reporting that identifies you. We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal data, because none of it ever leaves your device through us.

Map tiles (the one external connection)

When the map shows the OpenStreetMap base layer, your device requests map image tiles directly from the OpenStreetMap tile servers over HTTPS. As with any web request, that server receives your device's IP address and the coordinates of the map area being viewed. This is handled by OpenStreetMap, not by us, and is governed by the OpenStreetMap Foundation privacy policy. The PDF maps you import are rendered entirely offline and involve no network connection.

Permissions

  • Location (while using the app): to show your position and record trails. The app does not use background location.

Children

Inukshuk is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children under 13.

Your control and rights

Because all of your maps and tracks live only on your device, you are in full control: delete individual items in the app, revoke the location permission in your device settings, or uninstall the app to erase everything. There is no server-side copy for us to delete.
