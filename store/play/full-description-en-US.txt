Inukshuk is a free, open-source trail companion that works where the signal doesn't. Bring your own georeferenced maps, see your live GPS position on them, and record every outing — all completely offline, with nothing tracked and no account required.

Built for hikers, skiers, paddlers, hunters and backcountry explorers who already have the maps they trust and just want them to come alive in their pocket.

WHY INUKSHUK

• 100% free — no paywall, no premium tier, no ads, ever.
• Open source (MIT licensed) — anyone can read the code, audit it, and contribute.
• Community-driven — features and plugin ideas come from the people who actually hit the trails.
• Private by design — your location never leaves your phone. No analytics, no tracking, no sign-up.

BRING YOUR OWN MAPS

Import georeferenced PDF maps — park maps, topographic sheets, ski-hill trail maps, marine charts, anything with embedded geo-coordinates — and Inukshuk overlays your live GPS position right on top. The map you already rely on becomes a live navigation tool.

WORKS WITH NO SIGNAL

Everything runs on your device. When you wander past the edge of your imported map, an OpenStreetMap base layer fills in so you're never looking at a blank screen. No data plan needed on the trail.

REAL 3D TERRAIN

See the landscape around you in true 3D relief, with your saved trails and live recording draped over the mountains — a genuinely useful way to read the terrain ahead, not just a gimmick.

RECORD AND SHARE YOUR ROUTES

Hit Record to capture your trail with live time, distance, and elevation gain/loss. Every recording is saved and can be shared as a standard GPX file you can open anywhere. Import GPX trails from friends or the web just as easily.

ELEVATION PROFILES, NOTES & PHOTOS

Every recorded trail gets a pace-and-grade elevation profile you can scrub through point by point. Drop notes and attach photos along the route to remember the lookout, the tricky crossing, or the perfect campsite.

ORGANIZE YOUR LIBRARY

Keep your maps and trails tidy with folders, and group the ones you need for a trip into a bundle you can activate in a single tap.

FOREGROUND-ONLY LOCATION

Inukshuk only uses your location while the app is open and on screen — nothing runs in the background, nothing drains your battery behind your back.
