Everything offline. Nothing tracked.

Inukshuk turns your own georeferenced PDF maps into a live navigation tool — no data plan, no account, no servers watching where you go.

🗺️

Bring your own maps

Import georeferenced PDF maps and see your live GPS position right on them — park maps, topo sheets, ski trails, anything.

📡

Works with no signal

Everything runs on-device. An OpenStreetMap base layer fills in when you wander off the edge of your map.

⛰️

Real 3D terrain

See the landscape around you in true 3D relief, with your trails and route draped over the mountains.

📍

Record your routes

Track live time, distance and elevation gain/loss. Save and share every outing as a standard GPX file.

📈

Elevation profiles

Every recorded trail gets a pace-and-grade profile you can scrub through, with notes and photos along the way.

🔒

Private by design

Your location never leaves your phone. No analytics, no ads, no account. Read the privacy policy →

Up and running in a minute

Install & allow location

Open the app and grant foreground location so it can show where you are.

Import a map or GPX

Tap Import and pick a georeferenced PDF map or a GPX trail from your device.

Head outside

Navigate offline and hit Record to capture your trail. That's it.
